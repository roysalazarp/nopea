Here’s how we work with you.

We start by getting to know your specific business needs.

We want to understand which key business metrics you want to improve, by how much, and why they matter to your business.
We also want to understand what metrics we can measure along the way to determine we’re getting closer and closer to the desired outcome.
We want to hit a home run for your business—what would be the ideal business outcome you’d like to see once the project is completed?

Next, we send you a proposal with multiple options.

The proposal will include three fixed pricing options, with each package building on the previous one and listing the key metrics we’ll work on.

You choose one option and proceed with payment.

Payment is made 100% upfront, but if you are not satisfied with our collaboration within the first 30 days, we can call it off, and we will refund your money in full.

We then set up communication and collaboration channels.

We’ll be in touch regularly to evaluate and measure results along the way, which will also help us understand how to calibrate our strategy to ensure we hit a home run.

In some cases, we may find it beneficial to conduct interviews with key members of your team.

We present to you an outline of our approach to improving your key business metrics.

We will be intentional in selecting only those tasks we are confident will contribute to improving your key business metrics and, ultimately, achieving the desired business outcome. This also means we don’t take on task requests that don’t clearly support these objectives, ensuring our efforts remain focused.

We begin implementation and measure progress based on your desired outcomes.

We aim to deliver the simplest possible version of the product as quickly as possible so you can start using it and we can measure whether we are on the right track. This version 1 should capture a significant portion of the project’s value, and as we continue refining the details, we will progress toward a final deliverable that fully meets the desired business outcome.

Once complete, we hand over the final deliverables.

We follow up to evaluate our solution’s contribution to your goals and provide free support as agreed.

Since we’ve been measuring improvements in your key business metrics along the way, we’re confident you now have what you need. However, we’ll remain available a little longer to ensure everything is working smoothly.
